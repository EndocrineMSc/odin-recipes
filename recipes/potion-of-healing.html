Weak Potion of Healing

Description

A typical, weak healing potion. Very little and inexpensive ingredients are needed for this potion.

If brewed correctly, it will heal minor wounds, such as scrapes on the knee, mild headaches or a bite of a small animal.

The consistency should be slightly viscous but still watery. The final color should be a bright red.

We don't talk about the taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 Terraria
  • 1 Waterbloom

Steps

  1. Crush 1 terraria in the mortar and add it to the cauldron
  2. Also, crush 1 waterbloom in the mortar and add it to the cauldron
  3. After that, add 1 terraria to the cauldron (do not crush)
  4. Stir well using water to adjust the mixture

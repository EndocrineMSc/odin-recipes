Potion of Invisiblity

Description

A standard potion of Invisiblity. It is only semi-useful since you can't see anything when you become invisible, so don't even think about anything pervy and/or criminal.

The blue liquid of the final product should be constantly emit slow moving bubbles and be viscous, similar to an seed oil.

Smell and taste of this potion are unremarkable.

Ingredients

  • 2 Witch Mushrooms
  • 5 Windblooms
  • 2 Waterblooms

Steps

  1. Crush 2 witch mushrooms in the mortar and add them to the cauldron
  2. Next, crush 2 windblooms in the mortar and add them to the cauldron
  3. Add 1 waterbloom to the cauldron (do not crush)
  4. After that, add 1 windbloom to the cauldron (do not crush)
  5. Crush 1 windbloom in the mortar and add it to the cauldron
  6. Add 1 windbloom to the cauldron (do not crush)
  7. Then, add 1 waterbloom to the cauldron (do not crush)
  8. Stir and boil until perfect viscosity is achieved
